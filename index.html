The Challenge

Problem Statement

Car owners and car traders struggle to manage vehicles through their full lifecycle.

No centralized vehicle tracking

Vehicle information is often spread across different tools, notes, and systems.

01

Scattered maintenance records

Service history becomes difficult to follow, verify, and present when needed.

02

Poor expense visibility

Owners cannot easily understand total cost or make informed financial decisions.

03

Difficult resale preparation

Incomplete records and missing images slow the selling process and reduce confidence.

04

Manual management processes

Repeated manual work creates delays, errors, and dependency on technical support.

05
The FlipFix approach FlipFix solves this by creating one connected vehicle lifecycle.
